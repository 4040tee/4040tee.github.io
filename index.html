About Me

Hi there, welcome to my website! I hope you learn about my technical skills and get a glimpse at who I am as a person.

I am a problem solver, creative thinker, and continuous learner. I just finished my third year of Computer Science and Applied Mathematics and have gained experience in web and mobile development, game development, distributed and parallel developing for Big Data, and networking. These experiences were gathered from classes, internships, and by attending Hackathons all around North America. I love taking every opportunity I can to learn and discover what knowledge is out there.

When it comes to my personality, I have always loved stepping out of my comfort zone and trying new things. I am very creative so I have always loved different art forms, like drawing, sculpting, graphic design, and photography. I also grew up doing many different sports, where I have had the chance to build friendships and improve my team work and collaboration skills. In addition, I am passionate about leadership and helped advocate for and assist over 100 students by being a Student Representative.

In my free time, I enjoy traveling, drawing, playing volleyball, going for long drives with friends, and trying new foods.
